Types of Home Health Care Services

Home Health Care is a wide range of medical services provided to patients in the comfort of their homes. The focus of Home Health Care is to deliver ‘Healthcare outside hospitals’ in the right way. Home Health Care provides clinical excellence and superior patient experience at an affordable cost.

Types of Homecare Services

Depending upon the patient’s condition, there are a wide range of Home Health Care Services that one can receive at home. This can range from primary nursing care to specialized medical services, which include the following:

Doctor Care

With today’s hectic lifestyle, doctor service at home has become the preferred option. Doctor care is where the doctor comes home to the patient to diagnose and treat any illness. The doctor reviews the health status, suggests diagnostics, prepares a treatment plan, and refers them to a specialist if required. Doctor on call service saves a lot of time, and it is very convenient to make a booking online.

Who will benefit

  • Patients suffering from seasonal flu and acute diseases
  • Patients suffering from chronic conditions like Bronchitis, diabetes, heart disease and hypertension.
  • Elderly and bed-ridden patients who find it difficult to travel to the hospital

    • Nursing Care

    Nursing care is the most common and extensive care a person can get outside a hospital. Nurses are assigned to set a care plan and assistance after doctors’ consultations. This might vary according to the patient’s condition and health care needs. Nursing care includes medical monitoring such as medical reminding, checking sugar levels, blood pressure and temperature. They also include therapies such as speech therapy, occupational therapy, physiotherapy, etc.

    Who will Benefit:

  • Nursing care at home can be vital for people suffering from progressive neurological conditions such as brain stroke, spinal cord injuries, dementia, terminal cancer, etc.
  • Older adults who are weak and need assistance with daily activities.
  • Post-pregnancy care for mother and baby.
  • To provide injections and infusions to patients.

    • Occupational Therapy

    Occupational therapy deals with helping patients with physical, developmental, social, or emotional disabilities and regain their basic functionalities.

    Who will Benefit:

  • Kids and teens who have birth injuries and congenital disabilities.
  • Patients suffering from mental health or behavioral problems
  • Patients suffering from Autism
  • Patients who have gone through traumatic injuries
  • Post-surgical conditions

    • Speech Therapy

    A speech therapist helps a patient with impaired speech, regain their ability to communicate clearly.

    Who will Benefit:

  • Children with speech disabilities like stammering, speech delay and problems in producing sounds.
  • Patients with hearing impairment
  • People suffering from eating and swallowing problems. People suffering from neurological impairments and degenerative conditions, including stroke, head injury, Parkinson’s disease, and dementia.

    • Physiotherapy Services

    Physiotherapists are health care professionals who can help patients regain their mobility, strength and functionality and reduce pain using different techniques such as massage, exercises, and movements. Home Healthcare Services provides physiotherapy services in the comfort of your home.

    Who will Benefit:

  • People suffering from back or neck pain
  • People who are facing difficulty in walking
  • People suffering from joint and muscle pain
  • People suffering from mobility issues post-injury or surgery.
  • Pregnant women for posture and easy delivery
  • People suffering from stiff necks and shoulder tightness
